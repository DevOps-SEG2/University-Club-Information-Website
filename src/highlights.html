Major Achievements

These are the defining moments that put our university clubs on the national map.

2025

National Hackathon — 1st Place

The App Development Club represented the university at the National Innovation Hackathon, competing against 80 universities across the country. The team built an AI-powered campus safety app and secured first place with a PKR 500,000 prize.

🥇
2025

Inter-University Debate Championship — Champions

The Parliamentary Debate Society brought home the national championship title for the second consecutive year, with three members qualifying for the international WUDC competition in Seoul.

⚽
2024

University Football League — Regional Champions

The Football Club went unbeaten across the regional circuit with a 9-0 win-loss record. Captain Bilal Tariq was awarded Best Player of the Tournament and signed a trial with a national-level club.

🔐
2024

National CTF Competition — 2nd Place

The Cybersecurity Society placed second in the 48-hour national Capture The Flag competition, competing against 120 university teams. The team is now partnered with a leading cybersecurity firm for internship placements.
